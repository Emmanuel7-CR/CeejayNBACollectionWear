About CEEJAYNba Collection Wears

Brand History

At CEEJAYNBA Collection Wears, we're excited to embark on this fashion journey. Our brand was founded on October 2024 by Yoweibo Christopher, a passionate entrepreneur with a vision to provide comfortable, stylish, affordable, and high quality clothing to fashion enthusiasts.
